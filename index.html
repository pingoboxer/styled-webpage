Nigerian Soups

Nigerian soups are diverse, flavorful, and culturally significant, with examples like Okra, Banga, and Edikang Ikong, typically served with starchy staples.

Nigerian Swallow

Nigerian "swallow" are starchy foods like fufu or eba, eaten with soups, providing a neutral base to balance the rich flavors.

Rice

Nigerian rice dishes range from the popular smoky Jollof to vegetable-filled fried rice, aromatic Ofada, and simple white rice, showcasing diverse flavors and ingredients.
